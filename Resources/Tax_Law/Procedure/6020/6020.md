# I.R.C. § 6020 – Preparation of Returns by Secretary

**Text:** (a) Preparation of Returns by Secretary. – If any person fails to make any return required under any internal revenue law at the time prescribed therefor, or makes, willfully or otherwise, a false or fraudulent return, the Secretary shall make such return from his own knowledge and from such information as he can obtain through testimony or otherwise.

**Key Points:**
- Authorizes the IRS to prepare a substitute return when taxpayer fails to file.
- Often leads to a "notice of deficiency" and assessment of tax plus penalties.
- Taxpayer can still file a correct return later to replace the substitute.
