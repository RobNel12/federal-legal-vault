# I.R.C. § 6072 – Time for Filing Returns

**Text:** Except as otherwise provided in this subtitle, returns made on the basis of the calendar year shall be filed on or before the 15th day of the fourth month following the close of the calendar year.

**Key Points:**
- Sets the standard filing deadline (April 15 for calendar-year taxpayers).
- Extensions available via Form 4868 (individuals) or Form 7004 (corporations, etc.).
- Different deadlines for fiscal year taxpayers and certain entities.
