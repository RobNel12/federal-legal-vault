# I.R.C. § 6011 – Requirement of Return

**Text:** Except as otherwise provided in this subtitle, any person made liable for any tax imposed by this subtitle, or with respect to whom there is made available a refund, shall make a return or statement according to the forms and regulations prescribed by the Secretary.

**Key Points:**
- General requirement to file a tax return when liable for tax.
- Applies to individuals, corporations, partnerships, estates, trusts, etc.
- Failure to file can lead to penalties under § 6651(a)(1).
